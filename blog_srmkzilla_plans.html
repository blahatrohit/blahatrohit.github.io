Future Plans for Srmkzilla

Over the past year the Srmkzilla community has seen tremendous change. We have seen our team work hard and contribute to the benefit of the society.

Srmkzilla was started with the notion of showing the “Power of web” to the students of SRM university. It seems that it has achieved much more than that.
The year 2017 is an important year for the club as the Mozilla Tamilnadu community is organizing its first ever meetup where the contributors across tamil nadu will be attending.
It gives me great pleasure sharing what I have in mind for my club.

Events under Srmkzilla in the year 2016

• Mozofest – A Srmkzilla Fest.
• Saturday Chill Outs - where interested students of srm university attend a session on a topic presented by students, having expertise on the respected field.
• Elluminate 2.0 - an event in association with Safe India Orphanage, Villivakkam, aimed to better the lifestyle and knowledge of under privileged children.

Plans for 2017

• Introducing the members of the Mozilla team to technologies like WebVr, and rust.
• Reaching out to more students of the college through activities like Saturday chill outs.
• Inviting other Mozilla contributors to SRM university to talk and share their views and knowledge on the different aspects of technologies that the team can contribute in.
• Conduct the second edition of mozofest in Srm.
• Completion of atleast 2 projects on add-ons or any other open source technology.
