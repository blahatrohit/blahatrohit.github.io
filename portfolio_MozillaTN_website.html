Website Design for Mozilla Tamilnadu

Mozilla Tamilnadu is a group of Contributors who are passionate about Mozilla related Technologies and products. I was a part of the community and was asked to redesign the community website.

On December 1, 2016 Mr Viswaprasth put up an invitation on the MozillaTN blog for Invitation for Redesigning MozillaTN website.
 As a part of the community I took up the invitation and designed a website keeping the requirements in mind.


PHASE 1: Understanding the requirement

The requirement from the client side was short and dint offer much details.

PHASE 2: Research, Analysis, Gathering Concepts

Most of my inspiration came in from the Mozilla website, the simple and intuitive UI of the website was best suited for the community’s needs.
To make it more relatable to the Mozilla UI I decided use the exact same colours as used by the official website.
• Priorities
1. List of the latest posts by contributors
2. Space to Share Latest Firefox download link.
3. Space to share our Social Links.
4. Share important resources links.

PHASE 3: Low fidelity Wire framing and prototyping

With the requirements in mind and the client was presented the wireframe design for the website.

PHASE 3: High fidelity Wire framing and prototyping

To test the usability of the new design the high fidelity wireframe was developed.

PHASE 4: UI Design

The delivery of the final product was made on 3rd December 2016, while the pitched date of the delivery was on or before 15th December 2016

Conclusion

The design was met with overwhelming response from everyone and to this date standout as the cleanest UI delivered. Check out the other responses by contributors HERE
