Website Design for Srmkzilla

Srmkzilla is a club officially recognised by the mozilla organisation based in Srm university in Chennai, they mainly concentrate on educative activities and social endeavors and through their activities aimed at showcasing the “Power of web”
Being the club lead I took the opportunity to teach the members of the club how develop a website following the Software Development Lifecycle and also on how to deploy the website on the web.
I started of by explaining the requirements for the project followed by explaining in detail how the website needs to be developed step by step. I stitched together a team of 3 who were incharge of the development of the website. I took charge being the project manager and provided deadlines and resourced necessary.
